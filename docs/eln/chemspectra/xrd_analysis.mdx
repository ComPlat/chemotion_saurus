## 1. Supporting file formats
For XRD analysis, we are now only support [JCAMP-DX files](https://iupac.org/what-we-do/digital-standards/jcamp-dx/) (*.jcamp, *.dx, *.jdx), zip (in [BagIt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BagIt) format) files.

## 2. Upload your data and view it
To upload and view your data, you can follow [this instruction](../analysis#2-analysing-data-in-chemotioneln)
