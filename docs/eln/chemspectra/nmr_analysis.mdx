## 1. Supporting file formats
For NMR analysis, we are now only support [JCAMP-DX files](https://iupac.org/what-we-do/digital-standards/jcamp-dx/) (*.jcamp, *.dx, *.jdx), zip (Bruker FID and [BagIt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BagIt)) files.

## 2. Supporting NMR layouts
We are now support 1H, 13C, 19F, 31P, 15N and 29Si NMR

## 3. Upload your data and view it
To upload and view your data, you can follow [this instruction](../analysis#2-analysing-data-in-chemotioneln)
