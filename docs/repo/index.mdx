Chemotion Repository: https://www.chemotion-repository.net/

The [Chemotion Repository](https://www.chemotion-repository.net/) is an open-access platform for managing, publishing, and sharing research data in the chemical sciences. It enables researchers to store experimental results, molecular data, and reaction information in a structured and reusable format.

## Overview of the Landing Page

The landing page serves as the main entry point and provides quick access to the repository’s core features:

- **System Information Banner**: Positioned at the top and dismissible by users, the banner highlights key topics and provides contact access for users to get support when needed.
- **Navigation Bar**: Located at the top, it provides access to key sections such as Data Publications, Molecule Archive, News, and user-specific areas, including Review and Embargoed.
- **Search Functionality**: A prominent search bar allows users to explore the repository by chemical methods, author name, or molecular structure.
- **Latest Publications**: The central area highlights recently published samples and reactions, providing direct access to newly released research data.
- **Statistics Panel**: A statistics overview displays the number of samples, reactions, and analyses, grouped by status: published, under review, and under embargo.
- **Molecule Archive**: The left section shows the number of reference samples available in the Molecule Archive and provides quick access to it.
- **Features**: This section summarizes the repository’s main features. The **Peer Review** section provides access to the Review Guidelines and reviewer information.
- **Partners and Affiliations**: The bottom section displays the logos of the partners and affiliations of the repository.

## Getting Started

New users can begin by exploring publicly available data or using the search function. Registered users can log in to upload, manage, and publish their own datasets.

We recommend reviewing the following sections:

- [Details and Standards](repo/details_standards) – describes the overall idea of how to deal with data in Chemotion repository.
- [Settings and Preparation](repo/settings_preparation) – offers guidance on preparatory steps that help ensure data completeness and facilitate a smoother publication process.
- [Publication Workflow](repo/workflow) – explains the repository’s publication workflow.
