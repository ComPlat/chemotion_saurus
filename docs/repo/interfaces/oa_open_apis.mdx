## Open APIs, a REST-style service that provides programmatic access to the metadata.

The below APIs are provided by the Chemotion Repository to enable programmatic access to the data. The Open APIs are designed to be simple and easy to use, and are based on the REST architecture. They are publicly accessible without the need for authentication:

- JSON-LD download
- Published molecule
- Published molecule list
- Published reaction
- Published reaction list

Explore the capabilities of our API effortlessly by referring to the [API documentation](https://www.chemotion-repository.net/swagger_doc).
