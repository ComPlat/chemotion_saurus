A **Dummy** field serves as a placeholder to occupy space within a column and remains invisible to users. This can be a helpful strategy in certain template designs.

<div align="center">
  <img src={fieldDummy} alt="LabIMotion - Layout with Dummy Field" />
  <FigCaption text="Dummy field type" />
</div>

For example, in the following design,

<div align="center">
  <img src={fieldDummyDesign} alt="LabIMotion - Design with Dummy Field" />
</div>

two Dummy fields will be created and will occupy the space after the "Half cell separation" field.

<div align="center">
  <img src={fieldDummyLayout} alt="LabIMotion - Design with Dummy Field" />
</div>

To create a **Dummy** field, simply click the <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faCirclePlus} /> button next to every field heading to add it.

<div align="center">
  <img src={fieldDummyCreation} alt="LabIMotion - Create Dummy Field" />
</div>
