title: Statistical Tests

After checking the assumptions for parametric tests (see the *Assumptions* section),  
we can proceed with an **Analysis of Variance (ANOVA)** to evaluate whether the predictors have an effect on CO<sub>2</sub> uptake.

The results indicate that both the CO<sub>2</sub> concentration and the treatment (*chilled* vs. *non-chilled*)  
have a statistically significant effect on CO<sub>2</sub> uptake.

In contrast, no significant interaction between concentration and treatment is observed.  
This suggests that the effect of CO<sub>2</sub> concentration on uptake is similar for both plant groups.
