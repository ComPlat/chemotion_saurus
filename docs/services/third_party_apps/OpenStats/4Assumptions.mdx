In the *Assumption* tab several tests can be conducted to check whether the assumptions for the parametric tests are met.
One possibility is to create a diagnostic plots as shown below.
The results indicate that the assumptions for parametric models are reasonably satisfied.

- Residuals are approximately normally distributed.
- Variance is relatively stable across fitted values.
- No extreme outliers or influential observations are present.

Therefore, a parametric model is appropriate for this analysis.
