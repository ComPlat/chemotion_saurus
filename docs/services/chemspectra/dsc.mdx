title: Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC)
---

:::info[Supported file formats]

For DSC analysis, we currently support [JCAMP-DX files](https://iupac.org/what-we-do/digital-standards/jcamp-dx/) (\*.jcamp, \*.dx, \*.jdx), zip (in [BagIt](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BagIt) format) files.

:::

1. To upload and view your data, you can follow [this instruction](../chemspectra#using-chemspectra).

2. Once your data is uploaded, you can manually edit values such as melting point and other thermal parameters by interacting with the dedicated fields.
   ![chemspectra dsc](/img/chemspectra/dsc/chemspectra_dsc.png)
